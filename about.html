    • ABOUT MYKL



    I’m a photographer with a natural, cinematic style, driven by an observational approach that focuses on form, light, and authenticity. My work is about connection; helping people and brands express their identity with confidence, honesty, and strength.


    Whether I’m working with a vibrant New Zealand business or creating a portrait that captures an individual’s story, I aim to reflect genuine character and emotion. Every project is a collaboration built on trust, curiosity, and shared purpose.

    Based in Hawke’s Bay, I often travel for commissions across New Zealand, photographing everything from commercial campaigns to creative personal projects. What motivates me most is celebrating people; their journeys, achievements, and the craft that shapes their work.
